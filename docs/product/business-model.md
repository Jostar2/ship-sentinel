# Business Model

## Initial model

Start as a service business with standardized outputs.

## First buyer

- agencies first
- founders and internal teams second

## Core offer

- scenario design
- direct hands-on testing
- evidence capture
- spreadsheet audit pack
- release memo

## Packaging logic

- fixed-scope packages for predictable buying
- optional rush fee for launch-critical checks
- paid re-test after fixes
- agency-friendly delivery bundles that can be passed through to client projects

## Why this monetizes

- launch risk has immediate budget
- spreadsheet outputs feel concrete and easy to hand around
- agencies can resell QA audit as part of delivery
- founders will pay to avoid public breakage during launch
