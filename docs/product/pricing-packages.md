# Pricing Packages

## Lite

- 5 critical scenarios
- one spreadsheet audit pack
- one release memo
- best for solo founders

## Standard

- 10 to 15 scenarios
- full bug register
- evidence pack
- release memo plus fix priority
- best for small SaaS teams

## Release Gate

- pre-release audit
- re-test after fixes
- final ship / fix / block memo
- best for launch week or client handoff
